What is Feng Shui?

Feng Shui is a set of principles originating from ancient China that involves applying common sense and instinct when you plan your spaces or build a home.
It is based upon the idea that your future path depends on luck, your efforts and mind state, or the natural and built environment, and that the practice of it is about optimizing your environment for the different parts of your life.
